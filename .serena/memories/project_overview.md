Crypto Signal Bot for Binance USD-M futures. Purpose: event-driven signal generation, tracking, reporting, and diagnostics for crypto markets; not order execution by default. Stack: Python, asyncio, Polars, SQLite/aiosqlite, structlog, aiohttp, WebSocket market data, Telegram delivery. Architecture centers on EventBus, SignalBot orchestration, BinanceFuturesMarketData for public market data, MemoryRepository for persistence, SignalTracker for lifecycle tracking, and startup/session reporting under data/bot/session/reports. Key roots: bot/application/, bot/core/, strategies/, tasks/, plus root modules like market_data.py, tracking.py, delivery.py, startup_reporter.py, config.py.
